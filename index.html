ABOUT PBNPS

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide young learners with an exceptional foundation for life. We strive to create a nurturing and engaging environment where small pupils can explore new ideas, utilize innovative technology, and develop their critical thinking skills. We are dedicated to supporting each child in becoming knowledgeable, compassionate, and ready to tackle any challenges that come their way

Vision Statement

Our vision is to build future leaders through sustainable basic education that empowers pupil's with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to address the complex challenges of our time.

Goals

  • Deliver a comprehensive curriculum that fosters critical thinking and creativity.
  • Integrate environmental awareness into all aspects of learning.
  • Promote social and emotional learning to develop empathy and resilience.
  • Encourage community engagement through collaborative projects.
  • Nurture ethical leadership and social responsibility among students.
  • Embrace diversity and inclusion in our educational approach.

Recreational Activities

Prestige Bilingual Nursery and Primary School fosters holistic development through diverse recreational activities like sports and arts, ensuring children thrive academically and socially

Sporting Activities

Prestige Bilingual Nursery and Primary School promotes physical fitness and teamwork through various sporting activities, including soccer, basketball, and athletics, helping children develop essential skills and a love for active living

School Buses

Prestige Bilingual Nursery and Primary School provides safe and reliable school bus services to ensure convenient transportation for pupils. This service supports parents by facilitating easy access to the school, promoting punctuality and enhancing overall pupils safety

Skills Development

Prestige Bilingual School emphasizes skills development by integrating foundational literacy and numeracy with essential soft skills such as creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration

History of Prestige Bilingual Nursery and Primary School

Founded in 2005, Prestige Bilingual Nursery and Primary School has been dedicated to providing quality education in a nurturing environment. The school was established with the vision of fostering bilingualism and cultural awareness among young learners. Over the years, we have expanded our curriculum to include innovative teaching methods and modern technology, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for future challenges.

With a commitment to excellence, Prestige Bilingual Nursery and Primary School has grown into a vibrant community where students thrive academically and socially, guided by a team of passionate educators.
